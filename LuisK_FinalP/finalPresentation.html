Relevant Background

Steps in Statistical Bleaching Modeling

  1. What is your question? Can a Bayesian approach identify combinations of environmental parameters predictive of Hawaiian Coral Reef Bleaching?
  2. What model of the world matches your question? Probably WHOLLY non-linear, but test linear first
  3. Build a test: glm or stan_glm -> family=binomial(link=“logit”)
  4. Evaluate test assumptions: Good Question
  5. Evaluate test results: Chisq and Logit Coefficients
  6. Visualize: Maps.. for now

Datasets

  1. Diver Surveys (Transect and Photo):
    • Site
    • Depth
    • Habitat
    • Health Status
  2. Remote Sensing (MODIS, VIIRS, WindScat, AVHRR, etc.):
    • Sea Surface Temperature (SST)
    • Photosynthetically Available Radiation (PAR)
    • Wind Speed
